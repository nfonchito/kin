How Kin works

You decide
how much Kin does.

Kin doesn't spend your money or make decisions without your permission — unless you tell it to. You're always in control.

💡
Suggest Mode
Default for new tasks
Kin drafts every action and waits for your approval before doing anything. You review, tap confirm, and it executes. Nothing happens without your say-so.
Kin drafts grocery order → you confirm → placed
Kin finds lawn care slot → you approve → booked
Kin suggests reschedule → you tap yes → done
⚡
Auto Mode
For trusted recurring tasks
For tasks you've approved before, Kin executes automatically and just notifies you when it's done. You set the rules — Kin follows them every time.
Weekly HEB order placed every Wednesday
Lawn booked automatically every 3 weeks
Dog walker scheduled when travel is detected

Privacy & trust

Your family's data
stays your family's.

Kin knows a lot about your family. Here's exactly how that information is protected, stored, and controlled.

🔐
End-to-end encryption
All family data — schedules, preferences, conversations — is encrypted at rest and in transit. Nobody at Kin can read your family's information.
🎛️
You control what's stored
Delete any memory at any time. Turn off learning for specific categories. Export your full data in one click. Your data, your rules.
💳
Spending limits you set
Set per-transaction and monthly caps. Kin never spends above your limit without a second confirmation from a family admin.
👤
No human reviews your data
Kin is fully automated. No Kin employee ever reads your conversations, schedules, or family information for any reason.
🚫
Never sold, never shared
Kin's business model is your subscription — not your data. We don't sell, share, or use your information for advertising. Ever.
🧒
Built for families with kids
Children's data is handled with extra care. No profiling of minors. All child-related data is siloed and never used for any external purpose.
